Summer 2014 Visualization Group

We're a group of six undergraduate students at CU Boulder and two high school students. The goal this summer has been to develop a teaching tool for transcriptional regulation. The teaching tool will cover key concepts (through a video) and then allow students to learn interactively (through an app). The level of detail is gauged for an AP High School or Introductory College level class.

David Knox

David Knox is a PhD student in the Computational Bioscience program.

His research interests include:

Modeling transcriptional regulation, transcription factor binding, nucleosome formation. His research is focused on being able to predict transcription events based solely on DNA sequence and DNA binding factor concentrations.
