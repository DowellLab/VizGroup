Summer 2014 Visualization Group

We're a group of six undergraduate students at CU Boulder. We've split into two teams for the summer: the app team and the video team. The purpose of both is to teach AP High School level students more about transcriptional regulation.

David Knox

David Knox is a PhD student in the Computational Bioscience program. His research interests include: Modeling transcriptional regulation, transcription factor binding, nucleosome formation. His research is focused on being able to predict transcription events based solely on DNA sequence and DNA binding factor concentrations.
